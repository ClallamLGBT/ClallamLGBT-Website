About

ClallamLGBT is a collective of people from Forks to Blyn. We are the Lesbian, Gay, Bi, Trans, Queer & Questioning, Intersex, Asexual/Aromantic/Agender, Two-Spirit (LGBTQIA2S+) eyes and ears of Clallam County. We are not incorporated. We are a grassroots efforts to establish recognition within our larger communities
